Contact

Questions, corrections, and feedback.

Send feedback, corrections, feature ideas, or data questions to mlbstatline@gmail.com.

Please include the page, player, team, season, or matchup you were looking at if you are reporting a possible data issue.

Useful Feedback

What helps most.

If a number looks wrong, include the page URL, selected season or range, team filter, player name, and the source you are comparing against.

If something is confusing, a short note about what you expected to happen is often more useful than a full bug report.

If you have a feature idea, examples help: a player comparison, a team question, a fantasy use case, or a historical debate you wanted the site to answer.

Response Notes

Small project, real inbox.

MLB Statline is currently an independent project, so replies may not be immediate. Corrections that affect data clarity, broken pages, privacy, or source attribution get priority.
