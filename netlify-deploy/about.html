About

Baseball stats built for quick context.

MLB Statline is an unofficial baseball statistics tool for exploring season leaders, team context, player splits, Statcast boards, fantasy angles, news links, and batter-pitcher matchups.

The goal is to make public baseball data easier to scan and compare, especially across seasons, teams, leagues, and player matchups.

This site is not affiliated with Major League Baseball, MLB Advanced Media, Baseball Reference, Baseball Savant, or any MLB club.

How To Use This Site

Start with a question, then narrow the board.

Use Batting and Pitching to compare single seasons or year ranges, then narrow by league, team, position, or stat category.

Use Advanced and Statcast when you want rate stats, quality-of-contact context, and deeper player profiles beyond traditional box-score totals.

Use Matchups to compare a batter against a pitcher, review career head-to-head history, check pitcher handedness splits, and see how a whole roster has performed against that pitcher.

Use Splits for one-player breakdowns across seasons, and Fantasy when you want a practical board organized by fantasy needs.

Use Teams and News when you want a team-specific starting point for stats, links, communities, and current baseball headlines.
