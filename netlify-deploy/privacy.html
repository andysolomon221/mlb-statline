Privacy

Simple privacy for a simple stats tool.

MLB Statline does not currently require accounts, logins, payments, or personal profiles.

The site may use normal hosting logs from Netlify or similar infrastructure, such as page requests, browser type, device type, approximate technical information, and error or security signals used to serve and protect the site.

MLB Statline uses Google Analytics to understand broad site usage, such as page views, events, engagement time, traffic sources, and device categories. This helps identify broken pages, confusing layouts, and which tools visitors actually use.

The site does not sell personal information, run user accounts, collect payment details, or ask visitors to submit sensitive personal data.

The site links to third-party pages, including MLB, Baseball Savant, Baseball Reference, RSS publishers, team sites, and fan communities. Those sites have their own privacy practices.

If advertising is added later, ad partners may use cookies or similar technologies to measure and serve ads. This policy should be updated before ads are enabled.

You can use browser settings, privacy extensions, or analytics opt-out tools to limit tracking by third-party analytics and advertising services.

Privacy questions can be sent to mlbstatline@gmail.com.
