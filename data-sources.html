Data Sources

Where the numbers come from.

Player, team, season, roster, split, and batter-vs-pitcher data are loaded from public MLB Stats API endpoints.

Statcast leaderboards are loaded from public Baseball Savant leaderboard CSV data and paired with MLB team metadata where needed.

News pages use publicly available RSS feeds and links back to original publishers. This site does not republish paid articles.

Baseball Reference links are outbound search/profile links only. This site does not copy Baseball Reference statistical tables.

Because public data endpoints can change, numbers should be treated as a baseball research aid, not an official record or betting recommendation.
